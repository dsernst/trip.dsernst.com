Random Trip handles the hard parts — where to go, what everyone can afford, who can make it — so all you have to do is join.

how it works
The ritual

Simple by design.

01

Invite your people

Family. Old friends. Whoever you want to keep close. The group makes one commitment: we're meeting up somewhere new, regularly. That's it.

02

A destination gets picked for you

No voting. No debates. No one person stuck making the call. The destination is chosen based on where everyone is, what everyone can spend, and what your group has loved before. You find out together.

03

Whoever can make it, joins

Opt out of any trip, anytime, no explanation needed. Life happens. The ritual keeps going.

04

Leave a quick note after

A sentence or two from everyone who went. What clicked, what didn't. It quietly shapes the next pick.

Budget & location

Built around real life.

Everyone sets their own spending limit — no awkward conversations, no one dragged somewhere they can't afford. Everyone shares roughly where they are, so the destination actually makes sense for the group.

Closer trips

Easier to coordinate, cheaper to get to. Great for a long weekend when schedules are tight.

Farther trips

More travel time, more cost — and more of an adventure. Both have a place in the rotation.

Opting out

Committed to the ritual.
Not every trip.

Anyone can sit out any round, for any reason, at any time. No guilt. The group keeps the ritual going — whoever shows up, shows up.
